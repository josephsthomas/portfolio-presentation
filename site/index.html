LLM-AWARE SEO

Search is splitting into two channels: traditional rankings and AI-generated citations. I build for both. Schema markup tuned for entity recognition, structured data that maps to how models retrieve and cite sources, and content architectures that answer the intent patterns LLMs surface, not just the keywords Google ranks.

PROMPT LIBRARIES

Most teams using AI for content are improvising every prompt from scratch. Inconsistent output, no way to compare results. I build standardized prompt sets for content audits, audience analysis, and rendering reviews that any strategist can run and get reliable output. The prompts encode the methodology so the work scales.

HUMAN-IN-THE-LOOP

AI drafts fast but gets things wrong in ways that matter. Off-brand language, unsupported claims, regulatory violations. I design rubric-based scoring systems between AI output and publication. Each draft gets evaluated against accuracy, brand voice, and the compliance requirements of the client's industry.

AGENTIC WORKFLOWS

The full pipeline from draft to published asset. AI handles what it's good at, humans own what it isn't. AI generates drafts, subject matter experts check accuracy, MLR approves for compliance, and the asset publishes through defined handoffs. Every step has clear entry and exit criteria.
